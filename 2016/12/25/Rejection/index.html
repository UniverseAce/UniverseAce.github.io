Rejection

2016-12-25

Rejection can be a serious potential threat and makes me afraid.

I have to admit that sometimes I fear to be rejected. For example, before I asking some questions, I already predict the potential results. And sometimes, I anticipate really bad results. Thus, I start feel uneasy and even tired.

Today, I watched a TED talk about the rejection.

The speaker did a project about being rejected 100 days. And Finally, he said, we should not avoid being afraid of rejection. We can negotiate by asking why and talking about the doubt others have.

What we need is to communicate and keep engage. Find out the reason and what can be done to imporve it. This is the right way to conquer the fear of rejection.
